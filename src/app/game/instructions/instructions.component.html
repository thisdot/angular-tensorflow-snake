Welcome to Snake using Angular and Tensorflow!

This game demonstrates the power of combining Angular and Tensorflow to build highly interactive and reactive applications. To play, we need access to your camera as your hand is the controller!

How to Play

To control the snake, use only 1-hand (preferablly your right) and a single digit (thumb or index finger recommended) to point in the direction that you want the snake to go.
